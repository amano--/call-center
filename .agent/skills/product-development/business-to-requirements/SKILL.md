# Business to Requirements Skill

A skill for creating requirements definition documents from business requirements. Deeply understands client business challenges and transforms them into measurable, implementable requirements.

## When to Activate

This skill activates in the following scenarios:

- Conducting business challenge interviews with clients
- Analyzing current state and extracting issues
- Creating requirements definition documents
- Organizing functional and non-functional requirements

## Key Processes

### 1. Business Challenge Interviews

- Understanding client background
- Clarifying problems to be solved
- Grasping expectations and goals
- Confirming constraints

### 2. Organizing Requirements

- Defining functional requirements
- Confirming non-functional requirements (performance, security, etc.)
- Defining user personas
- Creating use cases

### 3. Creating Requirements Definition Documents

- Background and objectives
- Defining scope
- Detailed description of functional requirements
- Documentation of non-functional requirements
- Constraints and assumptions
- Success criteria definition

## Deliverables

- Requirements Definition Document (RFD)
- User Stories
- Use Case Diagrams and Descriptions
- List of Constraints
- Organized Questions and Clarifications
